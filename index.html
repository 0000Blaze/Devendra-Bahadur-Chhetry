About Me

Welcome to my page! My name is Devendra Chhetry and I am a Retired Professor from Tribhuwan University as Head , central department of statistics. I received my Ph.D. from University of Texas, Dallas, USA and have been working in the field of Statistics for more than 50 years. In my leasure time, I teach students studying M.Phil. . I have presented my work at numerous Conferences ,Symposia and Workshops. Outside of academia, I enjoy Travelling and am an avid fan of taking care of my health.
Thank you for visiting my page. Please feel free to contact me if you have any questions or would like to learn more about my work. My email is chhetrydevendra@gmail.com
